## Notes

- TSMC financial data is sourced from official investor relations quarterly earnings calls and annual reports. NTD to USD conversions use approximate average exchange rates for the reporting period.
- Foundry market share rankings are published quarterly by TrendForce and Counterpoint Research; figures may differ slightly between analysts due to methodology differences.
- EUV system allocation to TSMC is estimated from industry analysis since ASML does not officially disclose customer-specific shipment breakdowns.
- The 92% advanced node production share figure represents volume manufacturing capability at sub-7nm nodes and is widely cited across SIA, CSIS, and industry analyst reports.

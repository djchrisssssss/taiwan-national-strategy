## Notes

- Geographic measurements (strait width, depth, channel dimensions) are standard references confirmed across multiple authoritative sources including NGA, NOAA, and GEBCO.
- Shipping and trade transit figures derive from AIS vessel tracking data and UNCTAD maritime trade statistics; annual variation is expected.
- PLAN fleet counts and ADIZ incursion tallies vary by source and counting methodology. The DoD annual China Military Power Report is the primary U.S. government reference.
- The Gerald C. Brown ADIZ tracker compiles daily MND press releases into a structured dataset; it is the most widely cited independent compilation.

## Notes

- TeleGeography's Submarine Cable Map is the authoritative industry reference for submarine cable infrastructure. Cable counts may vary slightly depending on whether decommissioned or planned cables are included.
- The Matsu Islands cable cut incident (February 2023) is a well-documented case study in communications vulnerability. Both cables were severed within days, allegedly by Chinese fishing/dredging vessels. Repair took approximately 50 days.
- Starlink does not currently operate in Taiwan. The statistic is included for context on LEO satellite broadband as a potential resilience option.
- The satellite backup investment figure of $550 million is based on government budget announcements and forward-looking infrastructure plans; exact allocations are subject to annual budget adjustments.
- ICPC fault statistics cover global submarine cable incidents; the Asia-Pacific proportion is estimated from regional reporting.

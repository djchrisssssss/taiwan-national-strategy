## Notes

- EW capability details are among the most heavily classified aspects of defense planning. Specific system designations and technical parameters for Taiwan's indigenous EW programs are not publicly available.
- The PLA's Strategic Support Force (SSF) was reorganized in April 2024 into the Information Support Force. The exact number of EW brigades directed at Taiwan is an estimate from U.S. DoD and independent analysts.
- GPS spoofing/jamming incidents are documented through AIS vessel tracking anomalies, aviation incident reports, and academic radionavigation monitoring. Exact attribution to specific actors is often difficult.
- The 66 F-16V Block 70 aircraft figure represents new-build orders; an additional ~140 existing F-16A/B aircraft are being upgraded to F-16V standard with modern EW suites.
- Cumulative U.S. arms sales since 1979 exceeding $80 billion includes all categories, not solely EW-related systems.

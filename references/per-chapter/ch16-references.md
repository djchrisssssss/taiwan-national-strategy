## Notes

- Chapter 16 was reorganized in 2026-03-19 to focus exclusively on non-traditional security threats (UAP investigation and contact preparation). Former sections on controlled nuclear fusion and long-distance energy transmission were integrated into Chapter 5 (Energy Security). Former section on national blockchain infrastructure was integrated into Chapter 9 (Financial and Digital Resilience).
- The UAP investigation framework follows the institutional model established by the US AARO (established 2022 under DoD/DNI) and NASA's UAP Independent Study Team (report published September 2023).
- The contact preparation section applies the same low-probability/extremely-high-consequence scenario analysis framework used elsewhere in this document (e.g., full maritime blockade scenarios in Chapter 5).

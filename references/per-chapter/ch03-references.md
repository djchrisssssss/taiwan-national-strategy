## Notes

- Defense budget figures include both the base MND budget and special procurement budgets. When special budgets are excluded, the defense-to-GDP ratio is approximately 1.7-1.8% rather than 2.45%.
- IDS submarine specifications are partially classified; displacement and capability figures are from defense media reporting and CSBC Corporation unveiling data.
- DSCA arms sale notifications represent approved sale values, not actual delivery values. There is a substantial backlog of undelivered items estimated at $19-20 billion.
- The drone procurement figure of 3,400+ units spans multiple categories (attack, surveillance, loitering munitions) and evolves with annual budget allocations.
- Hsiung Feng missile specifications are estimated from defense publications and NCSIST exhibition materials; exact parameters are classified.
